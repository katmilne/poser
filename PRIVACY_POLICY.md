# Poser Privacy Policy

**Company:** Concurrent Space
**Last updated:** July 19, 2026
**Contact:** poser@concurrent.space

This Privacy Policy explains how Poser: Pose Camera ("Poser," "the App")
handles information when you use it. Poser is operated by Concurrent Space
("we," "us," "our").

## Summary

Your pose references, camera captures, stickers, and edits are stored locally
on your device. Poser does not upload this content to any server we operate,
does not require an account, and does not sell or share your personal data.
Three limited providers handle specific functions on our behalf: RevenueCat
(purchase management), Aptabase (optional, opt-out analytics), and Sentry
(crash reporting). None of them receive your photos, poses, or stickers.

## Local storage

Your photos, pose reference images, custom cutout stickers, tags, and edit
data (frames, stickers, text notes) are stored locally on your device. Poser
does not send this content to remote servers, and we do not have access to
it. Deleting an item inside the App removes that file from your device;
deleting the App removes all of it.

## Permissions

Poser requests the following device permissions, each used only for the
stated purpose:

- **Camera** — to show the live viewfinder and take photos.
- **Photos (Add Photos Only)** — write-only access used solely to save a
  photo you take, or a decorated image you choose to save, into your
  device's Photos library. Poser never requests, and cannot use, permission
  to read your existing photo library.
- **Photo picker (`PHPicker`)** — used when you choose a pose reference or a
  source photo for the sticker cutout tool. This system picker runs outside
  the App and hands over only the specific photo(s) you select; it does not
  grant the App broader library access.

You can review or revoke any of these permissions at any time in iOS
Settings → Poser.

## Third-party services

Poser uses three limited third-party providers, none of which receive your
photos, poses, stickers, or other content you create in the App:

- **RevenueCat** — manages subscription and purchase entitlements. RevenueCat
  receives an anonymous app-generated user identifier and purchase/
  transaction data from the App Store (product purchased, purchase date,
  renewal status). See https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.
- **Aptabase** — optional, privacy-first product analytics that helps us see
  which features are used (for example, that a photo was captured, or a pose
  was imported), reported as anonymous, low-detail events with no photo
  content, file paths, or exact timestamps attached. Analytics is on by
  default and can be turned off at any time in Settings — once off, no
  further events are sent. See https://aptabase.com/privacy.
- **Sentry** — crash and error reporting, always active (it has no in-app
  toggle). When Poser hits an unexpected error, Sentry receives a stack trace
  and basic device/OS diagnostics tagged with the general feature area (for
  example, "camera capture" or "photo library save") — never your photos or
  other on-screen content. Poser's Sentry configuration disables IP address
  and default personal-data collection and does not use session replay. See
  https://sentry.io/privacy/.

Apple also processes payment information directly as part of any App Store
purchase; we never see or store your payment details.

Poser does not use advertising networks or data brokers, and none of the
above providers are used for advertising.

## Data deletion

Because your content lives only on your device, you're always in direct
control of it:

- Delete a photo, pose, or sticker from within the App to remove it
  immediately.
- Delete the App to remove all locally stored App data.
- Manage or cancel your subscription through your Apple ID account settings
  (Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your device).

Since we don't hold a copy of your content or a personal profile about you,
there's no separate "delete my account" request to make with us — deleting
the App accomplishes this. RevenueCat retains purchase records per its own
policy, linked only to the anonymous identifier above.

## Children's privacy

Poser is rated for a general audience and is not directed at children under
13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, and — as
described above — Poser does not collect personal information from any user
in the first place.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new features or legal
requirements. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last
updated" date above and, where appropriate, an in-app notice.
